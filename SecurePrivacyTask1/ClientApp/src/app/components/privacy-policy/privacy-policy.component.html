Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we use cookies and handle personal data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience by remembering your preferences, login details, and to help us improve the website by collecting analytics data.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as authentication cookies for logging in.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our website and track user preferences.

Managing cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences by accepting or declining cookies when prompted on our website. You can also clear cookies at any time through our cookie management options.
